Autumn Show & Game Fair 2021

 
 
Oct 2021 ‘ 3rd Oct 2021

Venue: South of England Showground, Selsfield Road, Ardingly RH17 6TL

Opening Times: 9am-5pm (Saturday) 9am-4pm (Sunday)

For Covid Safety, numbers to this event are limited. You must know the contact details of all your party and by purchasing tickets you have agreed to sharing your details and your party’s details with NHS Test and Trace. NHS QR Codes will also be available for checking in at the gates. Full Covid-19 Safety Measures: Click here

Children under 16 go free if accompanied by an adult. A ticket is still required (see below).

Carers can attend free of charge. Please see https://www.seas.org.uk/info/visiting/ for details.
